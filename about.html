About Jenna

Home cook, food lover, and kitchen experimenter. I built this site to help you turn everyday ingredients into something amazing.

Hi, I'm Jenna!

Growing up the son of a mom and pop diner, I've always believed that great food doesn't require a fancy grocery run. What started as helping out my family with errands for the store grew into cooking, learning, and experimenting some of my best recipes! From classmates, to professionals, everyone who tried my food helped gain me courage to branch out in ways I didn't think I was capable of doing. I learned new cooking techniques, studied abroad under masters, and started my own social media to spread what I have learned to love over my lifetime. Come join me in our journey to make this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity as fruitful as we can take it to be!

This space is my way of sharing that spirit with you. I love sharing fresh, simple, and flavorful recipes that I and many close to me eat in our everyday lives. Type in what you have, and I'll help you find something delicious to make. No stress, no waste, just good food that makes you have something to look forward to after your job, school, etc. On a seperate note, feel free to tag me on any of my social media's (at the bottom of each page) with new recipes I should look at, or if you just want to share anything you found interesting about food through photos and videos! And remember to always Espresso self, because Wine not? You're one in a melon!
